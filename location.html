Location

Float coffee co will be mobile through July and August. Be sure to keep your eyes open for S.V. Rainbow in Desolation sound, anchored and brewing up our full array of hot and cold coffee.

When not in Desolation sound you may find us resupplying and moored in Lund Harbour, or Westview Harbour in Powell River. We're always open for business so come by, and say hi.
